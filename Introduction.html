My December 2020 Story

The month of December starts now, where the wind starts to feel colder than before. I always feel very nostalgic whenever this time came, like I would think my birthday just happened yesterday. At the very beginning of December I planned to buy a Nintendo Switch which I really want to have it since 2 years ago but I couldn't afford. Last December 14th I got to drive an L3000 van, accompanied by my uncle and 2 cousins who were also trying to learn how to. 2 days after that I got my driver's license and I decided to enroll to a driving school near to where I'm at.
